Cosmic Echo

Medium

Oil on Canvas

Dimensions

48 x 72 inches

Year

2023

Artist's Statement

'Cosmic Echo' is an exploration of the universe's silent symphony, a visual representation of the vibrations that connect all things. This piece delves into the profound beauty found in celestial chaos, capturing the fleeting moments of creation and destruction within the vast expanse of space. It's a meditation on our place within the cosmos, inviting the viewer to contemplate the infinite.

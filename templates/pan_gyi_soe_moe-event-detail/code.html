Nocturne: An Evening Exhibition

Date

October 26, 2024

Time

8:00 PM - 11:00 PM EST

Location

The Grand Gallery, New York

About the Event

"Nocturne" is a solo exhibition by Elias Vance, exploring the interplay of light and shadow in urban landscapes after dark. This collection of large-format photographs captures the silent narratives of the city at night, transforming mundane scenes into cinematic moments.

The event will feature a curated selection of 20 prints, a live Q&A session with the artist, and an opportunity to purchase limited edition copies of the exhibition book. Join us for an immersive experience into the nocturnal world.
